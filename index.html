About BiminiArt

BiminiArt is an independent digital art studio focused on printable wall art, instant download poster collections and decorative artwork for personal spaces.

Our collections include vintage-inspired prints, coastal artwork, botanical posters, typography designs, animal wall art and curated gallery wall sets.

Bimini Analytics

Bimini Analytics is a private desktop application used internally by BiminiArt to manage Etsy and Pinterest marketing workflows.

The application helps organize product marketing, Pinterest campaign creation, analytics workflows and content management for BiminiArt.
